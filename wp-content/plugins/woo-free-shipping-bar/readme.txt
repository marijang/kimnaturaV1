== Description ==
>[Try the Demo](http://new2new.com/?item=woocommerce-free-shipping-bar "Demo WooCommerce Free Shipping Bar") | [Documents](http://docs.villatheme.com/?item=woocommerce-free-shipping-bar/ "Documents") | [Pro Version](https://goo.gl/W6bfKC "Pro Version")

[youtube https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=mScU2ZOLYfE]

The increase in sales and traffic to the website is a very important for a website or an online store. Customers making a purchase usually enjoy free shipping method. Woocommerce Free Shipping Bar allowing:

– Display the total amounts of customer to reach minimum order amount Free Shipping system.

– Display banner notification program Free Shipping.

– Show message successful when customers purchase to enough minimum amount Free Shipping or amounts missing to reach Free Shipping.

– Display progress with percentage amount current.

### Important Notice:
- Plugin works based on WooCommerce plugin.
- It is released on WordPress.org and you can use plugin as free to build themes for sale.

### FEATURES

– Support users know how much they have purchased to receive free shipping

– Works on mobile! Turn on or turn off them.

– Custom message Anounce system.

– Custom message purchased.

– Custom message success.

– Custom message error.

– Set initial delay, time to display to show.

– Allow close progress bar.

– FAST. Progress bar is lightweight and built to scale, so it won’t slow down your site (which can hurt your Google rankings).

– Easy to use

1. Unzip the download package

1. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
